About

  I am a graduate student in the Quantitative Economics program at the University of Pittsburgh, and will be finishing my program soon at the end of April 2021. I graduated from the University of Pittsburgh in 2017 with bachelor's degrees in economics and geology.

  As an undergraduate, I studied abroad at Sophia University in Tokyo, Japan on a Nationality Room Scholarship and participated in research as a Pitt Honors College fellow.

  Since graduation, I have worked in various positions from urban gardener to expense report auditor, but I soon hope to be gainfully employed in the field of data analytics. In addition to economics and programming, I am passionate about plants, fish, and rocks.
